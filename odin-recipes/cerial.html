Cereal

Cereal is a common breakfast food made from processed grains, often enjoyed with milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of your favorite cereal
  • 1 cup of milk (or a dairy alternative)
  • Optional: sliced bananas, berries, or nuts

Instructions

  1. Pour the cereal into a bowl.
  2. Add the milk over the cereal.
  3. Top with sliced bananas, berries, or nuts if desired.
  4. Enjoy your breakfast!
